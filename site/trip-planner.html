How to Travel

You can travel by bus (Daewoo, Faisal Movers), hire a jeep, or book local guides once you arrive in cities like Gilgit, Skardu, or Swat.

Where to Stay

From 5-star hotels to cozy guest houses, northern Pakistan offers a variety of stays. Booking platforms like Booking.com and Sastaticket.pk can help.

Budgeting Tips

  • Travel in groups to split costs
  • Use local transport instead of private jeeps
  • Negotiate hotel rates during off-season

Packing Essentials

  • Warm clothes (even in summer, nights are cold)
  • Power bank and backup SIM
  • Cash (ATMs are not everywhere)
  • Water bottle, snacks, travel towel
